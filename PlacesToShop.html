Holland and Barrett

Health food shop. A bit pricey, but they have a lot of the less-mainstream ingredients you might need. If you sign up for their reward card you can earn coupons, and they always have things on sale.

Holland and Barrett Website

Robinsons Greengrocers

Local greengrocers, with a 10% student discount. A lot of seasonal produce, as well as imported fruit and veg. They also sell local eggs, chutneys, and bunches of fresh herbs. If you need fresh garlic root, lemongrass, or a bag of monkeynuts, this is your place! They also do delivery.

Robinsons Greengrocers Website

North Road Foods

Hard to miss with the guy shouting his deals in the front. Great place for cheap fruit and veg, as well as snacks like crisps.

North Road Foods' Facebook Page

Mother Earth

Located in the back of Durham's Indoor Market Hall, Mother Earth is the best place to get all your dry goods. They sell grains, pulses, dried fruits, spices, as well as extra things like vinegars and spreads. Basically a local and cheaper Holland and Barret!
